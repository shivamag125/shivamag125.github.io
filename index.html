Shivam Agarwal

I am an Engineer at Cisco and a researcher at IIIT-Delhi, where I work on natural language processing and geometric deep learning. I graduated at the top of my class from MIT, India in Bachelors of Electrical and Computer Engineering in 2021, and have had the privilege of learning from my experiences and mentors at Cisco, Midas-IIITD, Vanderbilt University and, Project Manas.

Throughout my research career, I have been fortunate to contribute to a wide range of research directions, including graph learning, time-series forecasting, quantitative finance and computational social sciences. I would be happy to discuss any project idea at the intersection of the above areas; feel free to reach out if you are interested!
